Profile

Hello, my name is John Rodriguez. I am an English teacher with 17 years of experience. I grew up in Naples, Florida (USA), where I graduated from Naples High School and earned several IT certifications, including A+, Network+, and Server+.

Throughout my career, I have taught students of all ages—from preschool to university—and also trained corporate groups and professionals in private online classes. I have worked with learners from Colombia, Mexico, Argentina, Chile, the USA, Spain, the Netherlands, Japan, and China.

Currently, I lead an online English program for adult professionals, offering courses in General English, Technical English, Exam Preparation, and adaptation training for newcomers to English-speaking countries. My goal is to help students communicate with confidence, improve their careers, and succeed internationally.
