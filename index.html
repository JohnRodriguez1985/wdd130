John Rodriguez | to WDD 130.

Hello, my name is John Rodriguez. I am an English teacher with 17 years of experience. I grew up in Naples, Florida (USA), where I graduated from Naples High School and earned several IT certifications, including A+, Network+, and Server+.

My Hometown: Floridablanca

My hometown is Floridablanca, a city in the department of Santander, Colombia. It is close to Bucaramanga, and together they form a large metropolitan area. Floridablanca is about 925 meters above sea level and is known for its beautiful parks. One important place is the Piedra del Sol, a big ancient rock with carvings made by the Guane people more than 1,000 years ago.

My Favorite City: Valencia

My favorite city in the world is Valencia, in Spain. It is the third-largest city in the country. I like it because it has a special mix of historic buildings and modern, futuristic architecture, especially the famous City of Arts and Sciences. It is a beautiful and interesting place to visit.
