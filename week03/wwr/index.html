History

Wildwater kayaking has a rich history that dates back to the early 20th century. Initially developed as a means of transportation and exploration, it quickly evolved into a popular recreational activity. Pioneers of the sport sought out challenging rivers and rapids, pushing the boundaries of what was possible in a kayak.
